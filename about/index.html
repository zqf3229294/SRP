About

Sunrise Frontier Advanced Materials is dedicated to delivering high-performance advanced material solutions for global industries, with a focus on spherical nanomaterials and related advanced oxide technologies.

Leveraging established manufacturing capabilities and production experience in China, we provide consistent product quality, dependable supply, and responsive technical support to customers worldwide. Our materials serve applications including electronics packaging, advanced ceramics, thermal management, and specialty industrial uses.

As our North American business continues to grow, we are expanding our local presence in the United States and plan to invest in U.S.-based manufacturing and technical support capabilities once market demand and customer partnerships reach the appropriate scale. This strategy is intended to enhance supply chain resilience, shorten delivery times, and provide closer collaboration with customers.

We are committed to becoming a trusted long-term partner by combining manufacturing expertise with customer-focused innovation.
