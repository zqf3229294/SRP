About

SRP advanced materials, presented for global customer conversations.

Suzhou SRP New Materials develops, manufactures, and sells submicron spherical alumina and silica powders. SFAM US can present this product and technical portfolio for North American customer development.

Company Profile

Founded in November 2015, SRP focuses on research, production, and sales of submicron spherical aluminum oxide and silicon oxide powders including 3N, 4N, and low-alpha product families.

  • Registered capital listed at RMB 24.68 million in the source document.
  • Headquarters in Suzhou Industrial Park.
  • Two production plants with 3500 metric tons of annual spherical oxide powder capacity.
  • Qingdao sales company for commercial support.

Development History

The timeline keeps the original history content but updates it to match the product-led structure.

2015

SRP was founded in November 2015.

2016

Successfully developed direct-combustion preparation technology for spherical alumina powder.

2017

Released high-purity submicron spherical alumina powder products.

2018

Obtained a Chinese invention patent for direct-combustion technology, and spherical alumina products were successfully applied in thermal materials.

2019

Released submicron spherical silica products for CCL and EMC applications, and established Qingdao SRP New Materials Co., Ltd.

2021

The first plant built a 1000 metric ton annual production line for submicron spherical oxide powders.

2022

Recognized as a national high-tech enterprise.

2023

Developed low-alpha spherical silica and spherical alumina powders.

2024

Low-alpha products received important customer certification in Korea and Japan according to the source document.

2025

The second plant built a 2000 metric ton annual submicron spherical silica powder production line.
